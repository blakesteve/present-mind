Privacy & Confidentiality

Notice of Privacy Practices (NPP)

It is my legal and ethical duty to protect the privacy and confidentiality of my clients. Notice of Privacy Practices is a document that describes how medical information about you may be used and disclosed, and how you can get access to this information. This document has been compiled based on federal and state laws, as well as regulations set forth by my profession.

It is important that you fully understand the content of this notice before we can proceed with therapy. I would like to encourage you to become familiar with this document and to write down any questions that you may have regarding it. We will review the Notice of Privacy Practices at your initial appointment, and you will have your opportunity to receive clarification.
